About Me

I’m Sairaj, a game designer focused on building immersive, player‑centered experiences. I care deeply about clarity, flow, and how moment‑to‑moment interactions feel.

I blend UX thinking with systems design and rapid prototyping. My work spans solo games, mobile UI, icon sets, and playful interactions — always with an eye for readability and polish.

Tools I enjoy: Unity, Figma, Blender. When not designing, I explore small experiments, iterate quickly, and ship clean, thoughtful interfaces.
