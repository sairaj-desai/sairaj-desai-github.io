About Me

I’m the kind of person who loves just watching the world the way people move, talk, or get lost in their own thoughts. That’s usually where i get my ideas. I’ve always found it fun that the wild, impossible things we can’t do in real life can come alive through games.

That’s where everything clicked for me turning crazy ideas into something you can play, feel, and laugh at with others.

And when I’m not making things, I’m probably deep into fanfictions or wandering outside, letting myself get kidnapped (just kidding, of course).
